The First Hii

It was a night neither of us planned for. You were sad from a breakup, and I was tired from exams — two people who probably should have been sleeping. But instead, we talked.

That first "Hii" opened something. We shared things we don't usually share with strangers. You thought it was just one conversation, one night, one person who would fade away by morning. But we kept going — talking like two crazy people who had somehow found each other at exactly the right moment.

That night planted a seed neither of us noticed at the time. What started as a distraction from our own pain quietly became something we both needed more than we knew.

"Some conversations don't end — they just keep growing into something beautiful."

— 7 November, the night it all started
